Colton In A Green Bronco:

Portfolio Home Weeklies Exercises The Final About Me

Digital Art, December 2022

Inspired by Temara de Lempicka's infamous autoportrait Tamara in a Green Bugatti, my digital art, Colton In A Green Bronco, explores self-portraiture through digital medium, utilizing visual elements and symbols to reference my interest and hobby of cars.

FUN FACT:

Growing up as a little kid, my dream job was always to be a car designer. A dream of mine is to someday fix up and restore a '72 Ford Bronco.
