Isolation Bubble:

Portfolio Home Weeklies Exercises The Final About Me

Site-Specific Installation Art, December 2021

Isolation Bubble explores the relationship between space and materials through the idea of every individual being confined to their own personal bubble of isolation through fear, anxiety, and worry coming out of a post-pandemic.

FUN FACT:

The peice is comprised of over three rolls of bubble wrap and plastic packaging materials.
