Zoinks:

Portfolio Home Weeklies Exercises About Me

Drawing, 2019

Inspired by Grant Wood's infamous painting American Gothic, my drawing, Zoinks, recontextualizes the infamous American pop culture artwork through a new 21st century lens, featuring infamous childhood American pop culture icons, Scooby Doo and his pal Shaggy "Norville" Rodgers.

FUN FACT:

Growing up, my favorite televison show and cartoon character has always been Scooby Doo. Zoinks is inspired by childhood nostalgia.
