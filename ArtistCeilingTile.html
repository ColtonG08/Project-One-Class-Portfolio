Artist Ceiling Tile:

Portfolio Home Weeklies Exercises The Final About Me

Acrylic Paint, 2019

Artist Ceiling Tile is an acrylic painting on a ceiling tile in my high school art classroom. Through this compositional narrative, I leave behind a work of art that represents myself and my relationship with art, for future generartions of young student artisits to experience.

FUN FACT:

The very ceiling tile still stands proud today, accompanied by many others in my high school art classroom at Fairview High School.
